The dish

A breakfast wrapped in a banana leaf that became a whole country's comfort food.

On the plate

Coconut rice

Rice steamed in coconut milk and pandan leaf — soft, fragrant, faintly sweet. The heart of the dish.

Sambal

A slow-cooked chilli paste, sweet-spicy and a little smoky. This is the part people argue about.

The crunch

Crispy fried anchovies (ikan bilis) and roasted peanuts for salt and texture in every bite.

The sides

A fried or boiled egg and cool cucumber slices to balance all that heat. Simple, perfect.
